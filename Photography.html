Photography


The header says it all - this page is all about Photography!
I love doing Photography and especially when it is combined with outdoors, especially mountains, i could it all day.
Even one day wouldn't be enough. Spending a week in the mountains with my camera would be awesome.
I have always been creative but as i also love traveling, owning a lot of art and craft equipment is too much stuff. Therefore my Olympus OMD- EM5 Mark II is a perfect companion.
My camera is small enough to carry in my backpack, we can do art together and enjoy outdoors too. My Photography on instagram
